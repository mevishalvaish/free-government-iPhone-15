Who Is Eligible?

The primary qualification route is through the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) and Lifeline. You likely qualify if your household income is at or below 200% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines, or if you participate in:

  • SNAP / Food Stamps
  • Medicaid / Medi-Cal
  • SSI (Supplemental Security Income)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance
  • WIC
